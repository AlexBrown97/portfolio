Creativity is intelligence having fun

When I discovered that software development was an industry that managed to combine both creativity with logic, flair with problem solving and artistry with structure, it was clear that this was the career for me. After graduating with a degree in English Literature and Theatre Studies, I directed my creativity towards a different industry and saw myself transition from theatre maker, to software maker.

I took this leap by enrolling onto Northcoders: an award-winning intensive coding bootcamp in full stack software development. I saw this as an opportunity to not only better my skill set and introduce me to a new industry, but also as a chance to really challenge myself by tackling something I had never done before. The risk paid off, as I am now regularly making front-end and back-end projects as a student at Northcoders, as well as implementing practices like Test Driven Development (TDD), paired and mob programming and version control with Github.

Alexandra Brown. 2020.
